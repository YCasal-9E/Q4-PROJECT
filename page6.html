What's Next for ICT?

As we prepare to enter Grade 10, we’re looking forward to taking our ICT knowledge to the next level. We expect to dive deeper into more complex concepts such as server-side scripting, data validation, and responsive web development. Learning how websites adapt across different devices will not only sharpen our technical skills but also teach us the importance of user-centered design.

We are also excited to explore dynamic content creation through JavaScript frameworks, and possibly learn how to integrate basic backend concepts. Collaboration is expected to become more essential in projects, and we hope to develop stronger communication and teamwork in ICT settings.

With a foundation already built in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, Grade 10 ICT will challenge us to become more independent problem-solvers, logical thinkers, and creative innovators in the digital space. It’s a journey we’re ready to face with curiosity and determination.
